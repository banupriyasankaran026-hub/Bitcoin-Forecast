Project Title : CryptoCast: Multi-Horizon Bitcoin Price Forecasting Using Deep Learning

Description : Bitcoin prices are highly volatile and influenced by complex temporal patterns. Accurate short-term forecasting is crucial for traders, analysts, and automated trading systems. Traditional statistical methods struggle to capture non-linear dependencies and long-term temporal relationships present in crypto price movements.This project focuses on building deep learning models that learn from historical price sequences to forecast Bitcoin prices over multiple future horizons.
Here model is designed, trained and compared to predict:
Next-day price (1D)
3-day ahead price (3D)
7-day ahead price (7D)

Set-up : Python,Pandas,NumPy,PyTorch,Deep Learning,Time Series Forecasting,CNN / RNN / LSTM / Transformer

Business Use Cases :

Crypto Trading Platforms: Short-term price forecasting signals

Algorithmic Trading Systems: Multi-horizon decision making

Risk Management Tools: Anticipate short-term volatility

Investment Analytics Dashboards: Predictive insights

Educational Platforms: Teaching real-world time-series DL
